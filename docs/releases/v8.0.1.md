# NOOP v8.0.1

A solid fix update on top of the Liquid Metal launch. Eleven fixes across all three platforms, most of them from your reports on the board. No new UI to relearn, just things working the way they should.

## Fixes

**Your last night's stats stay put.** After the early-morning rollover, resting heart rate, HRV and breathing rate could blank out under the "Last night" heading even though the data was there. They now carry over from the last night that actually has them, independent of the recovery score. (#980 family)

**Rest stops freezing on an old number.** When a night did not produce a fresh Rest score, Today and the Apple Watch could show the last scored night as if it were today's, so Rest looked stuck while everything else moved. It now shows an honest "no scored night yet" state instead of a stale number. (#977, #931, #939)

**The home-screen widget updates on its own.** It only refreshed while the app was open, so a sync that finished in the background left yesterday's numbers on the widget. It now republishes and reloads when a background sync completes. Thanks ruedigermunz. (#980)

**The Haptic Clock is readable again.** The buzzes ran too close together to tell apart on the wrist. The gaps between pulses, and between the digit groups, are wider now so you can feel the time cleanly. Thanks akoshumig-sketch. (#981)

**Android stops looping on a strap that will not pair.** A WHOOP 4.0 that connected and subscribed but never finished pairing could loop connect, drop, retry forever and drain the battery. It now gives up after a few tries and shows the re-pair guide instead of hammering the strap. Thanks julienranc. (#982)

**Your imported nights show up as sleep.** If you use NOOP without a strap and import from Health Connect (Fitbit and others), your sleep was being kept only as a nightly total, so the Sleep screen sat empty. It now stores the full sleep sessions, so imported nights appear like a normal night. Thanks Retsim. (#983)

**The full-day heart-rate trend is one tap away.** The whole-day view with the sleep and activity bands was still there but buried, so people thought it was gone. Tapping the heart-rate card on Today now opens it directly. Thanks ck090. (#979)

**Charging shows on the new Today screen.** The redesigned Today only showed the strap battery percentage, not whether it was charging. There is now a bolt on the battery ring and a "Charging" label, matching Settings, Mac and Android. Thanks djrBrasil. (#972)

**Deep Timeline scrolls back three days.** It was clamped to one day, so older heart rate was only reachable through the day-stepper. You can now drag back up to three days continuously. Thanks ryanbr. (#986)

**Oura ring: two real data bugs.** Blood-oxygen readings were being computed wrong and could show impossible values around 223 percent; they now read as the true percentage. And a corrupt timestamp in a full history dump could bank a reading dated 1970 or far in the future; those are now rejected. Thanks pipiche38. (#968)

## Under the hood

- The live decode skips building a debug-only hex string on the streaming path, so the 1 Hz stream and history offloads do a little less work. Thanks MrL0. (#969)
- The daily scoring skips a redundant per-day lookup on the common single-strap setup. Thanks MrL0. (#970)
